ABOUT US

The No.1 BBQ fast food chain in the Philippines was established on December 12, 2003 in Ilo-ilo City by no less than its founder, Edgar “Injap” Sia II. From a Hiligaynon word for Mr. Barbecue, Mang Inasal was established as a unique entry to the saturated grill restaurant industry. Its Chicken Inasal as the flagship product is a distinctively Pinoy chicken inasal stamp-grilling with charcoal, a marinade concocted out of local spices and herbs, bamboo sticks for skewers – is no doubt the best tasting chicken inasal in the country today.

Edgar “Injap” Sia II did not let negativity stop him from starting Mang Inasal. Today, he is a real estate mogul, in partnership with Jollibee’s Tony Tan Caktiong.

“The more the many-er” is Mang Inasal’s motto. As the Philippine’s fastest growing barbeque fast food chain, it serves chicken, pork barbeque and other Filipino favorites. Currently, there are 500 branches nationwide, including in Bohol, sharing one goal: to serve guests delightful meals.

What makes us Mang Inasal different as a fast food chain?

  • First fast food that has unlimited rice and soup
  • Uses coal instead of electricity
  • Cares for your health by grilling and using local herbs
  • Ambience that encourages customers to use hands while eating
  • Fast growing company with currently 500 branches and 10,000 employees
  • Well oriented and train crews that will serve the customer very well
  • Locally adapted food menus are offered to make the customer feel like they are at home
  • To make customers satisfied especially those who are "Kanin Lovers"
